{% trans "Facilities" %}

 

{% blocktrans %}The CEOM is located at the Stephenson Research and Technology Center (SRTC) in the Research Campus of the University of Oklahoma (http://srtc.ou.edu). The CEOM has approximately 2500 square feet office space and 2000 square feet laboratory space at the SRTC. It currently has three core laboratories and one field observation platform.{% endblocktrans %}

 

{% blocktrans %}The SRTC hosts several research laboratories, including the Advanced Center for Genome Technology, Institute for Environmental Genomics, Molecular Anthropology Laboratory, Health and Human Biology Laboratory, Biomedical Engineering Laboratory, Ecology Laboratory and the OU Supercomputing Center for Education and Research (OSCER). It has a meeting room with Tele-Conference facility and an advanced teaching and meeting room (CORE). The open-space design and diverse research groups at the SRTC provide a productive environment to pursue integration of bio-, eco- and geo- informatics.{% endblocktrans %}
